Autobiography

Hi! My name is Emmet and I am a software engineer with a serious passion for interactivity and data. I've worked for 2 years in the software industry, and am now currently finishing my masters in Computer Science full-time at Tufts University. Previously I have worked on improving and maintaining the build tools and installers for a large product line of audio effects plug-ins, refactored statically-sized C++ GUIs to be resizable, developed highly-optimized python scripts utilizing computer vision and input validation. finished difficult tackled long-standing bugs in codebases, worked on refactoring dynamic resizing of statically-sized GUIs, and learned much from my experiences along the way :).

While pursuing my degree, I'm found that I'm very interested in the possibilities with web-based audio application. In the music recording industry, the industry standard method typically is to use a standalone application as the main platform to record, make, and edit music. My belief is that there is a lack of robust web-based options for music creatives on the market, and that there will be eventually be a point when the market will shift towards preferring web-based options.
